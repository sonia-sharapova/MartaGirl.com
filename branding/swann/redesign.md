# Website Re-Design

## Swann Auction Galleries

As part of my work at Swann Auction Galleries, I contributed to the redesign of their digital presence, ensuring the website reflected the historic elegance and authority of this prestigious auction house.

## Details

- **Client:** Swann Auction Galleries
- **Role:** Designer, Art Director
- **Year:** 2024
- **Tools:** Figma, Adobe Creative Suite

The redesign focused on creating a seamless user experience while honoring the auction house's 80+ year legacy. We emphasized high-quality imagery, clear typography, and intuitive navigation to help collectors discover and bid on exceptional works.
